BROTHER HEALED OF COVID-19

My brother Kwame Appiah had been admitted to the ICU as he was seriously ill with the COVID-19 virus. The doctors were concerned as his condition had not improved after 10 days of antibiotics. I wrote to the Church for prayer and for healing. After a few days, Barry’s condition steadily improved. I continued to pray that Kwame would breathe on his own. Ater being in a medically-induced coma for 2 weeks, Kwame’s condition stabilized on his way to full recovery. The doctors told me that he was the only one thus far who had made it out of the ICU in that particular hospital. It is truly a miracle!

NEW JOB DURING COVID-19 AFTER 3 YEARS OF UNEMPLOYMENT

After 3 years of being unemployed, I finally got a job offer. Praise God! In times like this when almost all businesses are going under and employees are getting laid off due to the COVID-19 pandemic, God has granted me His favor. My wife has been blessed by the monthly revivals and mid-week services. She was actually the one who applied for the job for me. We got the phone call for the job offer in mid-June, and I started work the next day. This job will definitely make a difference in our financial situation. To God be the glory!

BLESSED WITH BABY GIRL AFTER 7 YEARS OF TRYING

My wife and I have experienced disappointments as we went to several fertility clinics and spent a lot of money only to have more miscarriages. For years, we watched as all our friends had success in conceiving. We became hardened over time and somewhat cynical. It felt as though our age was working against us. But I never believed God would let us down. We had countless prayer sessions upheld by a continued belief that God would deliver. I am thrilled to announce that after almost 7 years of struggle, loss, and pain, great joy has come into our lives! Our baby girl, Nhyiraba, was born on May 30, 2020. She is completely healthy. Thank You, Lord!
